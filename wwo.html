What We Offer



Pembina Trails Early College (PTEC) is a school that focuses on programming that prepares students for jobs in the Information Technology (IT) industry. Students with a passion for software development, cyber defence, coding and more have an opportunity to graduate high school with a Manitoba high school diploma, a post-secondary diploma and a head start in an exciting career.

Whether they are designing and testing future products, analyzing defects in software or driving quality improvements for global customers, PTEC graduates will contribute to creating a better future.

Starting in grade 9, students will take their core/compulsory courses at one of two Pembina Trails High Schools. They will take their accelerated high school courses at the division’s campus at the University of Manitoba’s, Smartpark.

In grade 11, their studies will continue at the Manitoba Institute of Trades & Technology (MITT) in either one of two streams:

• Cyber Defence & Cloud Administration OR • Software Developer

Pembina Trails School Division aims to provide innovative opportunities for our students. PTEC was created with the vision of equipping our students with the skills required to succeed in the highly competitive technology industry by teaching students an accelerated, relevant, hands-on curriculum with mentorship, and work experience. Graduates can continue their education at MITT, apply to university or step seamlessly into the workforce. Opportunities are endless.
